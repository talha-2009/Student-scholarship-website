CERN Technical Student Programme

A 4–12 month placement at CERN in Geneva for students in applied sciences, computing, and engineering (Bachelor's or Master's level, not PhD holders). Students work on real projects contributing to CERN's mission alongside top scientists and engineers, receiving a monthly net allowance of CHF 3,486 plus travel expenses and 30 days of paid leave annually. Applicants must be nationals of a CERN Member or Associate Member State, have completed at least 18 months of undergraduate study (3 semesters) in a relevant field, and remain enrolled as full-time students during the placement. The 2026-3 batch closed June 30, 2026; the next cycle reopens for applications on 6 August 2026 — check careers.cern for the updated vacancy listing and deadline at that time.
