Scholarship • Germany

DAAD STEM Study Scholarship

DAAD scholarship for high-achieving students from developing countries pursuing a tuition-free Master's degree in mathematics, computer science, natural sciences, or engineering at a German university. Covers a monthly stipend (~EUR 992), travel allowance, and insurance. Deadlines vary slightly by program/country — confirm exact date on the official DAAD database for your country.
