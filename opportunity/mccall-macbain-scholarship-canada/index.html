McCall MacBain Scholarship

Fully funded Master's or second-entry professional degree scholarship at McGill University; covers tuition, a monthly living stipend (~CAD 2,300), relocation grant, and leadership training (up to 30 scholars/year). Two deadlines for the 2027 cohort: 19 Aug 2026 for applicants outside Canada/the US, and 23 Sep 2026 for Canadian/US citizens and graduates. Application portal opens 1 Jun 2026, separate from and prior to McGill admissions.
