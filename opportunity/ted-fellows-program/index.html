TED Fellows Program

## Introduction

TED has built its reputation on giving powerful ideas a stage, and the TED Fellows Program extends that mission directly to individual innovators, entrepreneurs, and change-makers, offering a genuine speaking platform at TED conferences alongside mentorship and media training. Unlike a continuously open application, TED Fellows calls open only periodically, meaning timing and patience matter as much as the strength of a candidate's underlying work.

## About the Opportunity

Selected Fellows gain a speaking opportunity at a TED conference, joining a global network of past Fellows working across nearly every field imaginable, from science and technology to social justice and the arts. Because selection depends on having an already-impactful project or body of work, TED Fellows functions less as career development for early-stage ideas and more as amplification for genuinely established, demonstrable impact.

## Benefits

Fellows receive a speaking opportunity at a TED conference, mentorship, media training, and access to the global TED Fellows network.

## Who Can Apply?

Innovators, entrepreneurs, and change-makers with an impactful project or body of work can apply, either through nomination or self-application depending on the specific cycle.

## Fields of Study or Available Areas

TED Fellows spans innovation and multidisciplinary work broadly, welcoming candidates from any field where they can demonstrate genuine impact.

## Required Documents

Applicants need an application form, a project description, and supporting media illustrating their work.

## Application Process

Applications open periodically via the TED Fellows website; prospective applicants should watch for open call announcements rather than assuming a continuously available application.

## Deadline

No current deadline has been announced; interested applicants should monitor the official TED Fellows website for the next open call.

## Why You Should Apply

For innovators with a genuinely impactful project who want global visibility and a platform to reach a wider audience, TED Fellows offers a rare combination of media training, mentorship, and the reach of the TED brand itself.

For current status, visit the official TED Fellows Program page: https://www.ted.com/participate/ted-fellows-program
