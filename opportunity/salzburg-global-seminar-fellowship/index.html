Salzburg Global Seminar Fellowship

## Introduction

Rather than a single standing fellowship, the Salzburg Global Seminar Fellowship operates as a rotating series of specific seminar programmes, each convening professionals and emerging leaders from different sectors at multi-day gatherings in Salzburg, Austria to tackle global policy challenges through structured cross-sector dialogue. Because each seminar has its own topic, eligibility profile, and application or nomination process, finding the right fellowship really means browsing the current programme list to identify the specific seminar that matches an applicant's background.

## About the Opportunity

Participants join seminars addressing specific policy or leadership themes, ranging across topics that shift year to year based on current global priorities, gathering with professionals from other sectors and countries for multi-day intensive dialogue in Salzburg. The Seminar's decades-long history means participants join a genuinely established global network spanning past cohorts across many different programme areas, not just their own specific seminar.

## Benefits

Participants receive full or partial funding for travel and accommodation, depending on the specific seminar, along with access to a global multi-sector network of fellows built over decades of programming.

## Who Can Apply?

Professionals and emerging leaders relevant to a specific seminar's topic can apply, with exact selection criteria varying by individual programme rather than one fixed standard across all seminars.

## Fields of Study or Available Areas

Seminar topics span policy, leadership, and multidisciplinary themes broadly, shifting based on current global priorities and the specific focus of each programme cycle.

## Required Documents

Applicants need an application form, a CV, and a letter of motivation, though specific requirements can vary by individual seminar programme.

## Application Process

Applicants apply or accept nomination for specific seminar programmes listed on the Salzburg Global website, since there is no single unified application covering all programmes simultaneously.

## Deadline

Because each seminar runs on its own schedule, there is no fixed deadline; applicants should browse the current programme listings for specific dates.

## Why You Should Apply

For professionals seeking genuine cross-sector dialogue on global policy challenges, Salzburg Global's rotating seminar structure offers access to a well-established international network, provided applicants take the time to find the specific programme that fits their background.

For full details, visit the official Salzburg Global Get Involved page: https://www.salzburgglobal.org/get-involved
