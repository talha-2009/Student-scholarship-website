Schwarzman Scholars Program

A one-year, fully funded Master's in Global Affairs at Tsinghua University in Beijing, designed to prepare future leaders to understand China's role in the world. Covers tuition, travel, a laptop, health insurance, and a living stipend, alongside leadership training, mentorship, and extensive travel within China. Open to applicants of any nationality who hold (or will hold) a bachelor's degree, are 18-29 years old, and have strong leadership potential; no Chinese language requirement. Applications for the 2027 cohort opened April 8, 2026, and close September 9, 2026 at 3:00 PM EDT.
