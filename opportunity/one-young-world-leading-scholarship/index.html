One Young World Leading Scholarship

Introduction

Each year, One Young World gathers thousands of young leaders in a different global city for its flagship Summit, and the Leading Scholarship exists to make sure cost never becomes the reason a genuinely promising delegate cannot attend. For the 2026 edition, held in Cape Town, South Africa, the scholarship covers the full cost of participation, with explicit priority given to candidates from underrepresented countries and those attending a One Young World Summit for the first time.

About the Opportunity

Selected scholars join delegates from around the world for a multi-day Summit built around plenary sessions, workshops, and networking with prominent global leaders and fellow young changemakers. Because One Young World deliberately prioritizes underrepresented countries and first-time attendees, the scholarship functions as a genuine access mechanism rather than simply rewarding those with the strongest existing profile or network within the One Young World community.

Benefits

Scholars receive full delegate access to the Summit, hotel accommodation, catering throughout the event, and round-trip travel to Cape Town, covering essentially every major cost associated with attending.

Who Can Apply?

Applicants aged 18 to 35 with evidenced leadership and a genuine commitment to positive change can apply, with priority given to candidates from underrepresented countries and those who have not previously attended a One Young World Summit.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship spans leadership and social impact broadly, welcoming applicants working across any sector where they can demonstrate genuine leadership and commitment to positive change, from business and civil society to government and grassroots organizing.

Required Documents

Applicants need a completed application form and a personal statement addressing their leadership impact and the change they have worked to create.

Application Process

Applicants apply via the online scholarship form on the One Young World website, with selection conducted on a rolling basis rather than a single review after a fixed deadline, meaning earlier applications can be reviewed and decided sooner.

Deadline

The current application deadline is 31 October 2026, though because selection is rolling, applying well before this date can improve an applicant's chances given the ongoing review process.

Why You Should Apply

For young leaders who want access to a genuinely global platform and network but face real financial barriers to attending, the One Young World Leading Scholarship removes those barriers entirely while actively prioritizing exactly the kind of underrepresented voices the Summit most benefits from including.

For full application details, visit the official One Young World scholarship page: https://www.oneyoungworld.com/scholarship/leading-scholarship-2026
