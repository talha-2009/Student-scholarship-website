UESTC Chinese Government Scholarship

A Chinese government scholarship administered by the China Scholarship Council (CSC) for international students to pursue undergraduate, Master's, or doctoral programmes at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in Chengdu. Fully covers tuition fees, on-campus accommodation, comprehensive medical insurance, and provides a monthly living stipend (CNY 2,500 for undergraduates, CNY 3,000 for Master's students, CNY 3,500 for PhD students) for the full duration of the programme. Open to non-Chinese citizens under 25 (undergraduate) or 35 (Master's/PhD) with strong academic records and no prior CSC scholarship; UESTC is a top-ranked Chinese university specialising in electronics, ICT, and engineering. Applications for the 2027 intake typically open December 2026 and close around March 1, 2027 — apply through both the CSC online system and UESTC's international admissions portal.
