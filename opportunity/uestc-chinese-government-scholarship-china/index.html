Scholarship - China

UESTC Chinese Government Scholarship

## Introduction

The University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, based in Chengdu and known as UESTC, funds international students at every degree level through the Chinese Government Scholarship in partnership with the China Scholarship Council. Because the process runs through two separate systems simultaneously, the national CSC application and UESTC's own international student system, applicants need to complete both in parallel rather than assuming one submission covers everything.

## About the Opportunity

Scholars pursue bachelor's, master's, or PhD study at UESTC, a university with particular strength in electronic science, engineering, and technology, choosing between Chinese-medium and English-medium instruction depending on their language proficiency and preference. The dual-application structure means successful applicants have effectively been vetted twice, once by the national CSC system and again by UESTC's own admissions review, giving the university confidence in the quality of admitted scholars.

## Benefits

Scholars receive full tuition, free or subsidized accommodation, a monthly stipend of CNY 3,000 for master's students or CNY 3,500 for PhD students, and comprehensive medical insurance worth CNY 800 per year.

## Who Can Apply?

Non-Chinese citizens in good health can apply, under 35 with a bachelor's degree for master's applicants, or under 40 with a master's degree for PhD applicants, meeting language requirements of HSK Level 4 for Chinese-medium programmes or IELTS 5.5+/TOEFL 80+ for English-medium programmes.

## Fields of Study or Available Areas

UESTC's particular strengths lie in engineering, computer science, and technology, reflecting its focus as an electronic science and technology-oriented university.

## Required Documents

Applicants need the CSC Application Form, a notarized highest diploma, transcripts, a passport copy, a CV, two recommendation letters, a statement of purpose, a research proposal for master's or PhD applicants, a medical examination form, and a non-criminal record report.

## Application Process

Applicants apply through both the CSC Online Application System, using UESTC's agency number 10614, and the separate UESTC International Student Application System, submitting all required documents to both by the deadline.

## Deadline

The current application deadline is 1 March 2027.

## Why You Should Apply

For international students interested in engineering or technology and considering study in China, UESTC's scholarship offers comprehensive funding at an institution known specifically for its technical strengths, provided applicants carefully manage the dual CSC and UESTC application process.

For full details, visit the official UESTC scholarship information page: https://en.uestc.edu.cn/admission/scholarships_cost/university_scholarship.htm
