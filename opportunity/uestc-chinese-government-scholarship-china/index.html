UESTC Chinese Government Scholarship

## Introduction The UESTC Chinese Government Scholarship funds international students pursuing bachelor's, master's, or PhD study at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, a leading Chinese technology-focused university based in Chengdu. Delivered jointly with the China Scholarship Council (CSC), it requires a two-track application process — one through the national CSC system and one directly through UESTC itself — which is a common but easy-to-miss requirement across Chinese Government Scholarship programmes. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) / China Scholarship Council (CSC) - **Country of Study:** China (Chengdu) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** Bachelor's / Master's / PhD - **Duration:** Length of the chosen degree programme - **Official Website:** en.uestc.edu.cn ## Benefits - Full tuition - Free or subsidised accommodation - A monthly stipend of CNY 3,000 for master's students, or CNY 3,500 for PhD students - Comprehensive medical insurance (CNY 800/year) ## Eligibility Criteria - Be a non-Chinese citizen in good health - Be under 35 with a bachelor's degree, for master's applicants - Be under 40 with a master's degree, for PhD applicants - Meet language requirements for your programme: HSK Level 4 for Chinese-medium instruction, or IELTS 5.5+/TOEFL 80+ for English-medium instruction ## Required Documents - CSC Application Form - Notarized highest diploma - Transcripts - Passport copy - CV - Two recommendation letters - Statement of purpose - Research proposal, for master's/PhD applicants - Medical examination form - Non-criminal record report ## Application Process **Step 1.** Register on the CSC Online Application System (studyinchina.csc.edu.cn) using UESTC's agency number, 10614. **Step 2.** Separately register and apply through the UESTC International Student Application System. **Step 3.** Submit your notarized diploma, transcripts, passport copy, and CV to both systems as required. **Step 4.** Provide your two recommendation letters, statement of purpose, and research proposal (for graduate applicants). **Step 5.** Complete your medical examination form and non-criminal record report. **Step 6.** Submit all documents to both systems by the deadline (typically 1 March), since both applications are required. ## Important Dates - **Application deadline:** See the deadline listed for this opportunity (1 March 2027) ## Selection Criteria Selection weighs your academic record and its relevance to your intended field, the strength of your statement of purpose and research proposal (for graduate applicants), and your language proficiency meeting the specific requirements of your chosen medium of instruction. ## Tips for Applicants - Remember this requires two separate applications — CSC and UESTC directly — and missing either one means an incomplete application - Start gathering your notarized diploma, medical examination, and non-criminal record report early, since these can take time to obtain - Confirm whether your intended programme is Chinese-medium or English-medium, since language requirements differ significantly (HSK 4 vs IELTS/TOEFL) - Write your statement of purpose and research proposal with genuine specificity about your chosen field at UESTC, particularly given its strong electronic science and technology focus - Apply well before the 1 March 2027 deadline, given the volume of documents required across both systems ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for the UESTC Chinese Government Scholarship?** Non-Chinese citizens in good health, meeting age and academic requirements for their intended degree level. **Is it fully funded?** Yes — tuition, accommodation, a monthly stipend, and medical insurance are all covered. **Do I apply once or twice?** Twice — through both the CSC system and the UESTC International Student Application System. **What language score do I need?** HSK Level 4 for Chinese-medium programmes, or IELTS 5.5+/TOEFL 80+ for English-medium programmes. **When is the deadline?** 1 March 2027. **What degree levels are covered?** Bachelor's, master's, and PhD. ## Apply Now Register through both the CSC Online Application System and the official UESTC International Student Application System: https://en.uestc.edu.cn/admission/scholarships_cost/university_scholarship.htm
