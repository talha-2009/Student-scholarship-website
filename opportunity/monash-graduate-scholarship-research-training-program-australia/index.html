Monash Graduate Scholarship (Research Training Program)

## Introduction The Monash Graduate Scholarship, delivered through Australia's national Research Training Program, funds PhD and Master's by Research candidates at Monash University, one of Australia's largest and most internationally engaged research universities. Rather than a separate competitive application, Monash uses an "invitation to apply" model: you first secure a supervisor and submit an expression of interest, and only then are invited into the formal scholarship and admission process — a structure that rewards early relationship-building with potential supervisors. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** Monash University - **Country of Study:** Australia (Melbourne) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** PhD or Master's by Research - **Duration:** Standard PhD or Master's by Research duration - **Official Website:** monash.edu ## Benefits - Tuition fees paid in full - An annual living allowance stipend, typically in the AUD 37,000-38,000+ range - A relocation allowance of approximately AUD 2,000 - In some cases, an additional research allowance ## Eligibility Criteria - Be a domestic or international student admitted to a PhD or Master's by Research at Monash - Demonstrate a strong academic and research record - Not have previously held a government- or Monash-funded postgraduate research award for more than six months ## Required Documents - Academic CV - Official transcripts and degree certificates - A research proposal - English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL), where applicable - References ## Application Process **Step 1.** Identify a Monash supervisor whose research area matches your interests and contact them directly. **Step 2.** Complete an expression of interest with that supervisor's support. **Step 3.** If invited to apply, submit your full application through the Monash Graduate Research Admission and Scholarship system, including your CV, transcripts, research proposal, and references. **Step 4.** You are automatically considered for eligible scholarships as part of this same application — no separate scholarship-only form is required. **Step 5.** Monash reviews your admission and scholarship eligibility together and issues a combined offer if successful. ## Important Dates - **Application deadline:** See the deadline listed for this opportunity (Monash runs scholarship rounds through the year; confirm the current round) - **Programme start:** Aligned with your Monash admission offer Because the process begins with an expression of interest rather than a direct application, start well ahead of any published deadline. ## Selection Criteria Monash assesses your academic and research record, the strength and feasibility of your research proposal, and the willingness and availability of a supervisor to take you on, since the "invitation to apply" model makes supervisor buy-in central to progressing at all. ## Tips for Applicants - Start by contacting potential supervisors directly — this scholarship genuinely requires securing supervisor support before you can be invited to apply - Make sure your prior research award history is clear, since holding a similar award for more than six months previously affects eligibility - Prepare your research proposal to be realistic in scope for standard PhD or Master's by Research timelines - Confirm your English proficiency test scores meet Monash's requirements well before applying - Track Monash's current scholarship round dates directly, since they run more than one round per year ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for the Monash Graduate Scholarship?** Domestic and international students admitted to a PhD or Master's by Research at Monash. **Is the Monash Graduate Scholarship fully funded?** Yes — it covers tuition, a living allowance, and a relocation allowance. **Do I need a supervisor before applying?** Yes, an expression of interest with a supervisor's support is the first step. **Is there a separate scholarship application?** No, eligible candidates are automatically considered for scholarships within the standard admission process. **Can I apply if I've held a similar award before?** Only if you haven't held a government- or Monash-funded postgraduate research award for more than six months previously. **Are international students eligible?** Yes, both domestic and international students can apply. ## Apply Now Contact a prospective Monash supervisor and begin the expression of interest process on the official Monash scholarships page: https://www.monash.edu/study/fees-scholarships/scholarships/find-a-scholarship/monash-graduate-scholarship-mgs
