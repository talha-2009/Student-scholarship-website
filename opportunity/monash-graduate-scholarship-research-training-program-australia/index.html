Monash Graduate Scholarship (Research Training Program)

Introduction

Monash University's Graduate Scholarship, delivered through Australia's national Research Training Program, funds PhD and Master's by Research candidates at one of Australia's largest and most internationally engaged research universities. Unlike a separate competitive application submitted after admission, Monash uses an "invitation to apply" model built around securing a supervisor first, a structure that rewards early relationship-building with potential supervisors well before any formal application is submitted.

About the Opportunity

Candidates begin by identifying a Monash supervisor whose research area aligns with their interests, then work with that supervisor to develop an expression of interest before being formally invited to submit a complete application. This sequencing means the supervisor relationship, not the scholarship application itself, is the real gatekeeper for admission and funding, making early, genuine outreach to prospective supervisors the single most important step for any interested candidate.

Benefits

Scholars receive tuition fees paid in full, an annual living allowance stipend typically in the AUD 37,000 to 38,000-plus range, a relocation allowance of approximately AUD 2,000, and in some cases an additional research allowance to support project-specific costs.

Who Can Apply?

Domestic and international students admitted to a PhD or Master's by Research at Monash can apply, with a strong academic and research record expected, and applicants must not have previously held a government- or Monash-funded postgraduate research award for more than six months.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship covers PhD and Master's by Research study across all fields available at Monash, reflecting the university's broad research strength across disciplines.

Required Documents

Applicants need an academic CV, official transcripts and degree certificates, a research proposal, English proficiency test scores through IELTS or TOEFL, and references.

Application Process

Candidates first secure a supervisor and complete an expression of interest, then apply via the Monash Graduate Research Admission and Scholarship system once invited; eligible applicants are automatically considered for the scholarship as part of this same admission process.

Deadline

The current application deadline is 31 August 2026. Because the process begins with an expression of interest rather than a direct application, interested candidates should start well ahead of any published deadline.

Why You Should Apply

For PhD and Master's by Research candidates who want comprehensive funding at a major Australian research university, Monash's scholarship offers strong financial support built directly into the admission process, rewarding candidates who invest time in building a genuine supervisor relationship early.

For full application details, visit the official Monash scholarships page: https://www.monash.edu/study/fees-scholarships/scholarships/find-a-scholarship/monash-graduate-scholarship-mgs
