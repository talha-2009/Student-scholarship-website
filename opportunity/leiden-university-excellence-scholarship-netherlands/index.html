Leiden University Excellence Scholarship

A competitive scholarship for outstanding non-EU/EEA students admitted to a Leiden University Master's programme, awarded in three tiers: €10,000, €15,000, or full tuition fee waiver for one year (the scholarship covers tuition fees only, not living costs, so recipients must have separate funds for accommodation and living expenses). Open to non-EU/EEA nationality students applying for the February 2027 intake of any Leiden University Master's programme, with a GPA equivalent to a Dutch 8.0/10 or higher. Applications for the February 2027 intake are submitted via the Leiden University application portal with a deadline of October 1, 2026; a separate call exists for the September 2027 intake with a February 1, 2027 deadline. Applicants must apply for their Master's programme and the LExS scholarship simultaneously.
