University of Toronto Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship

Introduction

The University of Toronto's Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship is one of Canada's most prestigious undergraduate awards, named after the former Canadian prime minister and Nobel laureate, designed for incoming international students who show exceptional academic and personal achievement. Unlike scholarships requiring a separate application form, Pearson works through high school nomination, making the relationship between a student and their school's scholarship coordinator the real first step.

About the Opportunity

Nominated students are considered for the scholarship as part of their standard University of Toronto undergraduate admission application, with the university assessing exceptional academic and personal achievement through the same materials submitted for admission itself. Because there is no separate Pearson-specific form, the strength of a student's admission essays and transcript effectively is their scholarship application.

Benefits

Scholars receive full tuition, books, and incidental fees, plus a living allowance for all four years of undergraduate study.

Who Can Apply?

Incoming international undergraduate students of exceptional academic and personal achievement admitted to the University of Toronto can apply, with high school nomination required in most cases.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship covers all undergraduate fields available at the University of Toronto, since eligibility is based on overall academic and personal achievement rather than a specific discipline.

Required Documents

Applicants need their University of Toronto admission application, a high school nomination, transcripts, and a personal statement or essays.

Application Process

Applicants apply for undergraduate admission to the University of Toronto and are nominated by their high school for the scholarship; there is no separate Pearson application form.

Deadline

The current application deadline is 7 November 2026.

Why You Should Apply

For exceptional international students who secure their high school's nomination, Pearson offers comprehensive four-year funding at one of Canada's top universities, without the burden of an additional scholarship-specific application.

For full details, visit the official Pearson Scholarship page: https://future.utoronto.ca/pearson/
