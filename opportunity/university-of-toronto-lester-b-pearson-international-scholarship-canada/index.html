University of Toronto Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship

[2026 intake cycle CLOSED — deadline was 7 Nov 2025] University of Toronto's most prestigious undergraduate award for international students; covers full tuition, books, fees, and four years of residence support (~37 scholars/year). Requires school nomination (one student per school) followed by UofT admission application. The 2027 intake (Sept 2027 start) timeline is not yet officially announced; based on past cycles, expect school nomination ~Oct, scholarship application ~Nov 2026. Confirm on the official page.
