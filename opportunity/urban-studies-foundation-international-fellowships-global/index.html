Urban Studies Foundation International Fellowships

Funds a 3-9 month visiting sabbatical at a host university of the candidate's choice, for early- to mid-career urban scholars from the Global South to write up existing research for publication, under a chosen mentor. Deadline confirmed: 6 July 2026, 23:59 (UTC+1).
