Scholarship • Germany

DAAD Study Scholarship

DAAD scholarship covering a monthly stipend (~EUR 992), travel allowance, and health insurance for international graduate students pursuing a Master's at a German university in any field. Application is made via the DAAD portal (portal.daad.de); deadlines are set per program but commonly fall around 15 Oct 2026 for an October 2027 start.
