Internship • Switzerland

UNCTAD Internship Programme

Unpaid internships with the UN Conference on Trade and Development, mainly at its Geneva headquarters, typically lasting 2-6 months. Open to students enrolled in a graduate program (or in their final year of undergraduate study) in a related field. Applications are accepted on a rolling basis through the UNCTAD internship portal.
