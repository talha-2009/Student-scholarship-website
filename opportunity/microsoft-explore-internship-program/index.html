Microsoft Explore Internship Program

Introduction

Microsoft's Explore Internship Program addresses a real gap many students discover the hard way: most competitive tech internships want candidates with prior internship experience, leaving first- and second-year students with few genuine entry points into the industry. Explore is built specifically to fill that gap, giving early-career university students a structured, paid introduction to engineering, technical sales, business, and other roles at Microsoft well before they would typically qualify for a standard summer internship.

About the Opportunity

Participants join a rotational programme designed around exploration rather than deep specialization, giving early-career students exposure to different functions within Microsoft so they can make a more informed choice about which career path to pursue in later, more competitive internship rounds. The programme functions explicitly as a feeder into Microsoft's broader University Internship track, meaning strong Explore participants often have a real advantage when applying for standard internships in later years of their degree.

Benefits

Participants receive a paid salary, mentorship from Microsoft professionals, relocation support where applicable, and the potential for conversion into Microsoft's standard University Internship programme in a subsequent year.

Who Can Apply?

First- or second-year university students pursuing a degree relevant to engineering, technical sales, business, or another Explore track can apply, with the programme specifically designed for students who would not yet be competitive for Microsoft's standard internship pipeline.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

Explore spans engineering, technical sales, business, and other early-career tracks at Microsoft, giving students a genuine choice in which functional area to explore based on their interests and coursework.

Required Documents

Applicants need a resume and a transcript documenting their current academic standing and coursework.

Application Process

Candidates apply via the Microsoft Careers portal, selecting the Explore Internship listing for their region and area of interest, then submit their resume and transcript before progressing through Microsoft's assessment and interview process for the specific track chosen.

Deadline

Microsoft Explore Internship applications open on a rolling basis across different regions; applicants should check the official Microsoft Careers student page regularly for current application periods.

Why You Should Apply

For first- and second-year students who feel locked out of standard tech internships due to lack of prior experience, Explore offers a genuine, paid entry point specifically designed for that career stage, often serving as the first step toward a longer relationship with Microsoft.

For full details, visit the official Microsoft Careers student portal: https://careers.microsoft.com/students/us/en/usuniversityexplore
