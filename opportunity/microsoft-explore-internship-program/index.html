Microsoft Explore Internship Program

## Introduction The Microsoft Explore Internship Program is an entry-level rotational internship specifically designed for first- and second-year university students, giving early-career students a structured introduction to engineering, technical sales, business, and other roles at Microsoft before they've typically had access to standard internships. It functions as a feeder programme into Microsoft's broader University Internship track, making it a genuine early foothold for students still exploring which tech career path fits them. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** Microsoft - **Country of Study:** Global — locations vary by role and region - **Opportunity Type:** Internship - **Funding Type:** Paid - **Degree Level:** Undergraduate (first- or second-year students) - **Duration:** A structured rotational internship period - **Official Website:** careers.microsoft.com ## Benefits - A paid salary - Mentorship from Microsoft professionals - Relocation support, where applicable - Potential conversion to the standard Microsoft University Internship programme ## Eligibility Criteria - Be a first- or second-year university student - Be pursuing a degree relevant to engineering, technical sales, business, or another Microsoft Explore track ## Required Documents - Resume - Transcript ## Application Process **Step 1.** Apply via the Microsoft Careers portal, selecting the Explore Internship listing for your region and area of interest. **Step 2.** Submit your resume and transcript. **Step 3.** Complete Microsoft's assessment process, which may include online tests depending on the specific track. **Step 4.** Progress to interviews with the hiring team. **Step 5.** Successful candidates receive an offer specifying role, location, and relocation support details where applicable. ## Important Dates Microsoft Explore Internship applications open on a rolling basis across different regions. Please refer to the official Microsoft Careers student page for current application periods. ## Selection Criteria Microsoft assesses candidates based on academic performance appropriate to an early-career student, genuine interest in the specific track (engineering, technical sales, or business), and interview performance, since this programme is explicitly designed for students without extensive prior internship experience. ## Tips for Applicants - Apply as a first- or second-year student specifically, since this programme is designed for that early stage, unlike Microsoft's standard university internships - Choose the specific Explore track (engineering, technical sales, business) that genuinely matches your interests, since this shapes your rotational experience - Highlight coursework and any early projects or extracurricular involvement relevant to your chosen track - Treat this as a genuine exploration opportunity, since the programme is designed to help you discover which Microsoft career path fits you - Consider this a strong stepping stone toward Microsoft's standard University Internship programme in a later year ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for the Microsoft Explore Internship?** First- or second-year university students pursuing a relevant degree. **Is it paid?** Yes, it includes a paid salary and, where applicable, relocation support. **What roles does it cover?** Engineering, technical sales, business, and other early-career tracks at Microsoft. **Does it lead to future opportunities?** Yes, it can lead to conversion into the standard Microsoft University Internship programme. **Do I need prior internship experience?** No, this programme is specifically designed for early-career students without extensive prior experience. **How do I apply?** Through the Microsoft Careers portal, submitting a resume and transcript. ## Apply Now Apply through the official Microsoft Careers student portal: https://careers.microsoft.com/students/us/en/usuniversityexplore
