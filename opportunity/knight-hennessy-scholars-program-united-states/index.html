Knight-Hennessy Scholars Program

A fully funded graduate fellowship at Stanford University supporting up to three years of any graduate degree (e.g. JD, MA, MBA, MD, MS, PhD) alongside a structured leadership development program. Covers tuition and fees, a living and academic stipend, a travel stipend, and a one-time relocation grant. Open to applicants of any nationality, age, or field of study; applicants must apply separately but concurrently to Knight-Hennessy Scholars and to a Stanford graduate degree program. The application for the 2027 cohort opened June 1, 2026 and closes October 6, 2026 at 1:00 PM Pacific Time; the linked Stanford graduate program application is due by December 1, 2026 or the program's own deadline, whichever is earlier.
