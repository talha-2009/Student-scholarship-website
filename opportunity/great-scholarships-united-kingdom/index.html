Scholarship • United Kingdom

GREAT Scholarships

Joint UK government (GREAT Britain Campaign) and British Council awards for one-year postgraduate Master's study at 60+ UK universities. Each award is worth a minimum of £10,000 toward tuition (tuition discount, not full funding). DEADLINE VARIES BY UNIVERSITY — most 2026-27 deadlines fall between March and July 2026; check your target university's page directly via the official link.
