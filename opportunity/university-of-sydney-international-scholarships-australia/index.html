University of Sydney International Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships for international undergraduate and postgraduate (HDR) students, covering tuition and a living stipend (~AUD 42,754/yr) for research degrees. Research-degree deadlines run on a rolling basis: 11 Sep 2026 (Research Periods 1-2, 2027 intake) and 18 Dec 2026 (Research Periods 3-4). Separate undergraduate scholarship rounds may close earlier — confirm on the official page.
