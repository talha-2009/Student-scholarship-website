Scholarship - Australia

University of Melbourne Graduate Scholarship

## Introduction

The University of Melbourne Graduate Scholarship supports both domestic and international students of high academic merit pursuing a Master's by Research or PhD at one of Australia's top-ranked universities. Like several Australian research scholarships, it is integrated directly into the graduate research admission process rather than requiring a separate scholarship-only application, which simplifies the process for applicants but places real weight on how strong your admission application itself is.

## Opportunity Overview

- **Organization:** University of Melbourne - **Country of Study:** Australia (Melbourne) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** Master's by Research - **Duration:** Standard length of the chosen research degree - **Official Website:** study.unimelb.edu.au

## Benefits

- A living allowance stipend at a competitive annual rate - A full tuition fee offset - Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) for international students - A relocation grant

## Eligibility Criteria

- Be a domestic or international student of high academic merit - Be admitted, or in the process of applying, to a Master's by Research or PhD at the University of Melbourne - Meet the University's standard graduate research admission requirements, since scholarship and admission are assessed together

## Required Documents

- Academic transcripts - A research proposal - CV - References - English proficiency evidence, for international applicants

## Application Process

**Step 1.** Apply for admission to your chosen graduate research degree (Master's by Research or PhD) at the University of Melbourne.

**Step 2.** Within the same application, indicate your interest in the Melbourne Research Scholarship — no separate application is typically required.

**Step 3.** Submit your transcripts, research proposal, CV, and references as part of your standard admission application.

**Step 4.** The University reviews your application for both admission and scholarship eligibility together.

**Step 5.** Successful candidates receive a combined offer covering admission and scholarship funding.

## Important Dates

- **Application deadline:** See the deadline listed for this opportunity - **Programme start:** Aligned with your University of Melbourne admission offer

Because scholarship consideration is bundled with admission, apply as early as your intended programme allows.

## Selection Criteria

Selection is based on academic merit demonstrated through your prior transcripts, the quality and feasibility of your proposed research, and the strength of your supporting references, assessed as part of the standard graduate research admission process.

## Tips for Applicants

- Treat your graduate research admission application as your scholarship application too, since no separate form is typically needed - Make your research proposal specific and realistic for the length of a Master's by Research or PhD - Secure strong academic references well before the deadline - If you are an international applicant, confirm your English proficiency evidence meets the University's specific requirements - Apply early, since strong admission applications are assessed on a rolling basis in many departments

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Who can apply for the University of Melbourne Graduate Scholarship?** Domestic and international students of high academic merit admitted to a Master's by Research or PhD.

**Is it fully funded?** Yes — it covers a living allowance, tuition fee offset, health cover, and a relocation grant.

**Do I need a separate scholarship application?** No, it is typically considered automatically as part of your graduate research admission application.

**Are international students eligible?** Yes, and they additionally receive Overseas Student Health Cover.

**What degree levels are covered?** Both Master's by Research and PhD.

**How is selection made?** Based on academic merit, research proposal quality, and references, reviewed alongside your admission application.

Apply for graduate research admission and scholarship consideration on the official University of Melbourne scholarships page: https://study.unimelb.edu.au/scholarships
