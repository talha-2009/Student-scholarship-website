Scholarship - Germany

Heinrich Böll Foundation Scholarship

Introduction

The Heinrich Böll Foundation, closely associated with Germany's Green political movement, runs a scholarship program unlike most politically neutral funding schemes: it explicitly selects for students who share the foundation's commitment to sustainability, democracy, and equal opportunity, alongside strong academic performance. For students whose civic or political engagement already aligns with those specific values, this scholarship offers funding tied directly to that alignment rather than academic merit alone.

About the Opportunity

Scholars pursue master's or PhD study in Germany across any academic discipline, but selection weighs genuine, demonstrated engagement with green political themes as heavily as academic transcripts, distinguishing this scholarship from most others in Germany's scholarship landscape. Beyond funding, scholars join the foundation's broader scholar network, participating in seminars and programming connected to the foundation's political and civic mission.

Benefits

Scholars receive a monthly stipend that varies by level of study, a contribution toward health insurance, and, depending on individual circumstances, possible family or mobility allowances. Scholars also gain access to the foundation's scholar network and seminars, connecting them with others who share similar civic commitments.

Who Can Apply?

Both German and international students of any discipline are eligible, provided they genuinely support the foundation's green political goals around sustainability, democracy, and equal opportunity. International applicants typically need German language proficiency at B2 level or above, and master's-level funding requires a completed first degree.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship covers all academic disciplines, with eligibility determined more by an applicant's civic and political engagement than by their specific field of study.

Required Documents

Applicants need an application form, a CV, a motivation letter, academic transcripts, proof of social or political engagement, and two recommendation letters.

Application Process

Applicants apply directly to the Heinrich Böll Foundation via its online application portal, submitting all required documents by the fixed deadline. Because the foundation explicitly assesses alignment with its political and civic mission alongside academic credentials, applicants benefit from being specific and genuine in documenting their own prior engagement with sustainability, democracy, or equal-opportunity causes, rather than treating this section of the application as secondary to academics.

Deadline

The current application deadline is 1 September 2026.

Why You Should Apply

For students whose academic and civic interests already connect to sustainability, democracy, or equal opportunity, the Heinrich Böll Foundation offers funding that explicitly values that alignment rather than treating it as incidental. Among fully funded scholarships in Germany, this one stands out for its clear political and civic identity, making it a strong fit specifically for students who can point to genuine engagement with the foundation's core themes, not simply strong grades.

For full application details, visit the official Heinrich Böll Foundation scholarship page: https://www.boell.de/en/scholarships
