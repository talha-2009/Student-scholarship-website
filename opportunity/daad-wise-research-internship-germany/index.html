DAAD WISE Research Internship

Fully funded ~2-3 month summer research internship (May-Jul 2027) for Indian undergraduate students (5th-6th semester Bachelor's, or 5th-8th semester integrated/dual degree) in science, engineering, and mathematics at German universities/research institutes. Covers a monthly stipend (~EUR 831), travel allowance, and DAAD health/accident/liability insurance. Requires a written invitation letter from a German host supervisor before applying — this is the most selective step.
