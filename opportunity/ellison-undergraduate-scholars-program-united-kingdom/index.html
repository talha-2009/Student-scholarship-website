Ellison Undergraduate Scholars Program

## Introduction

The Ellison Institute of Technology's partnership with the University of Oxford has produced one of the newer, more generously funded undergraduate scholarships in the UK, built around a specific set of values, innovation, collaboration, and integrity, rather than academic excellence alone. Ellison Scholars go through a genuinely two-stage process spanning both EIT's own selection criteria and Oxford's standard undergraduate admissions, with EIT's final decision made only after an Oxford offer is confirmed.

## About the Opportunity

Unlike scholarships layered simply on top of university admission, Ellison Scholars undergo initial screening by EIT before applying to Oxford, become semi-finalists if that initial review goes well, and only receive final confirmation as scholars after successfully securing an Oxford undergraduate place through the standard admissions process. This structure means EIT is evaluating candidates on more than academic potential from the very start, assessing fit with its own institutional values before a student's Oxford outcome is even known.

## Benefits

Scholars receive full Oxford course fees and a living stipend covering accommodation and everyday expenses throughout their degree. Distinctively, the scholarship includes paid summer innovation-project internships with EIT itself, giving scholars applied project experience alongside their academic studies. Travel and conference expense coverage, plus arrival, visa, and healthcare support, round out a genuinely comprehensive support package designed to remove practical barriers for international students specifically.

## Who Can Apply?

Applicants need to meet the University of Oxford's standard entry requirements for their chosen course, including grades and English language proficiency, while also demonstrating outstanding academic achievement and genuine alignment with EIT's stated mission around innovation, collaboration, and integrity. Because these values are assessed directly through the application, candidates should be prepared to demonstrate them concretely rather than simply asserting them.

## Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship is open across all fields available at Oxford, since eligibility hinges on meeting Oxford's entry requirements for whatever specific course an applicant chooses, alongside EIT's own values-based criteria.

## Required Documents

Applicants need the Ellison Scholars application form, a portfolio documenting achievements such as awards, research, or volunteer work, written responses to diversity and inclusion questions, and a separate standard University of Oxford undergraduate application.

## Application Process

Candidates begin with the eligibility check and application on the EIT Ellison Scholars portal, submitting their portfolio and required essay or diversity responses. Those who advance to semi-finalist status then apply separately to their chosen Oxford course through the university's standard undergraduate admissions system. EIT conducts its final selection round only after a candidate's Oxford admission outcome is known, meaning strong performance in Oxford's own admissions process remains essential even for a candidate who impressed EIT in the earlier stage.

## Deadline

The current application deadline is 31 July 2026. Because the process involves both an EIT application and a separate Oxford admissions timeline, applicants should start the EIT portion well ahead of this date to leave adequate time for the subsequent Oxford application.

## Why You Should Apply

For internationally strong undergraduate applicants who want both prestigious Oxford study and a distinctive innovation-focused experience alongside it, few scholarships combine full funding with paid summer internships built directly into the programme. Among fully funded undergraduate scholarships at Oxford, the Ellison Scholars Program stands out for its explicit values-based selection and its practical, applied internship component, making it worth the additional application effort for candidates who genuinely connect with EIT's stated mission.

For full eligibility details and to begin an application, visit the official EIT Ellison Scholars page: https://eit.org/education-and-scholarships/undergraduate
