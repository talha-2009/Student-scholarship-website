Internship - Germany

Max Planck Research Internship

Introduction

Three of Germany's leading computer science research institutes, the Max Planck Institutes for Informatics, Software Systems, and Security and Privacy, run a joint internship programme that is genuinely open to exceptional students worldwide, with no German language requirement at all. For students, and even industry professionals, in computer science or related interdisciplinary fields, it offers a funded route into some of the strongest CS research environments in Europe, at institutions whose alumni and faculty regularly publish at the top venues in the field.

About the Opportunity

Interns work directly with researchers at one of the three participating institutes, typically for 12 to 14 weeks though longer placements are possible depending on the project and mentor availability. Applicants can optionally indicate a preferred mentor or specific institute when applying, giving motivated candidates some real influence over which research group they end up joining, rather than being assigned arbitrarily. The work itself is genuine research contribution, not shadowing, meaning interns are expected to engage seriously with open problems in their chosen area, whether that involves theoretical computer science, systems research, or security and privacy work specifically.

Benefits

Interns receive a monthly stipend intended to cover living costs in Germany, covered housing arranged by the hosting institute, and round-trip travel from their home residence to the hosting institute, removing what would otherwise be a significant cost barrier for students traveling from outside Europe.

Who Can Apply?

Exceptional bachelor's, master's, or doctoral students worldwide, or industry professionals, in computer science or related and interdisciplinary fields can apply, with no German language requirement whatsoever. The programme is genuinely highly competitive and expects an excellent academic record, since the three participating institutes recruit from a global pool of strong applicants each cycle.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The internship covers computer and information science broadly, spanning the specific research strengths of the three participating Max Planck Institutes, including areas like algorithms, systems, machine learning, and cybersecurity depending on which specific institute and mentor a candidate connects with.

Required Documents

Applicants need a CV, academic transcripts, contact information for at least one reference, and an optional motivation letter, which, while not mandatory, can meaningfully strengthen an application by explaining specific research interests.

Application Process

Applicants apply online at the official application portal, optionally indicating a preferred mentor or institute based on their own research interests. The summer internship deadline typically falls around 1 November of the prior year, though applications for other times of year may follow a different, more flexible timeline depending on individual mentor availability.

Deadline

Because this internship runs on a rolling basis with a notable summer-cycle deadline around 1 November, applicants targeting a summer placement should apply well ahead of that date, while those interested in placements at other times of year should contact the programme directly to understand current availability.

Why You Should Apply

For computer science students and professionals who want serious research experience at a top European CS institute without needing German language skills, this internship offers strong funding and genuine research involvement at institutions doing globally recognized work across theoretical and applied computer science. Among international research internships and global opportunities for computer science students specifically, few combine this level of institutional prestige with such broad international accessibility.

For full details, visit the official Max Planck internship application portal: https://apply.cis.mpg.de/register/internship
