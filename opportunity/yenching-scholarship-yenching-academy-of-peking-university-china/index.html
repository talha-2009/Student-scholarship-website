Yenching Scholarship (Yenching Academy of Peking University)

## Introduction The Yenching Academy of Peking University offers a fully funded two-year interdisciplinary Master's in China Studies, drawing a genuinely international cohort — international students make up roughly 75-80% of each class — making it one of the most globally diverse graduate programmes based in China. It suits graduates from any academic field who want structured, funded exposure to China through an interdisciplinary lens, rather than a narrowly disciplinary master's degree. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** Yenching Academy of Peking University - **Country of Study:** China (Beijing) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** Master's - **Duration:** Two years (first year guaranteed, second year renewable on good standing) - **Official Website:** yenchingacademy.pku.edu.cn ## Benefits - Full tuition - On-campus accommodation - A round-trip travel stipend between your home city and Beijing - A monthly living stipend - Medical insurance ## Eligibility Criteria - Hold, or be on track to complete by 31 August, a bachelor's degree in any field - Demonstrate strong academic performance, leadership or community engagement, and English proficiency - Applicants worldwide are eligible; international students comprise roughly 75-80% of the cohort - There is no strict age cutoff, though the average admitted age is around 23 - Mainland Chinese applicants follow a separate, undergraduate-only PKU pathway ## Required Documents - Online application - Transcripts - Degree certificate or proof of enrollment - A personal statement - Recommendation letters - English proficiency test scores, sent directly by the testing body ## Application Process **Step 1.** Optionally contact Yenching Academy faculty in advance to express interest in supervision or specific research areas. **Step 2.** Complete the online application in English via the Yenching Academy application portal. **Step 3.** Submit your transcripts, degree certificate or proof of enrollment, and personal statement. **Step 4.** Arrange your recommendation letters and have your English proficiency scores sent directly by the testing body. **Step 5.** Submit your complete application by the deadline (typically 1 December). **Step 6.** If admitted, begin the two-year interdisciplinary Master's in China Studies, with your second year renewable based on academic standing. ## Important Dates Please refer to the official website for the current cycle's specific application deadline, since Yenching Academy typically closes applications around 1 December but exact dates can shift. ## Selection Criteria Yenching Academy evaluates candidates on strong academic performance, demonstrated leadership or community engagement, English proficiency, and the overall fit and coherence of their personal statement with the programme's interdisciplinary China Studies focus. ## Tips for Applicants - Consider reaching out to Yenching Academy faculty ahead of applying if you have a specific research interest, since this is an available and encouraged option - Use your personal statement to explain genuinely why an interdisciplinary China Studies programme fits your academic and career goals - Arrange your English proficiency test early, since scores must be sent directly by the testing body - Highlight leadership or community engagement clearly, since this is an explicit selection criterion alongside academics - Note that maintaining good academic standing is required to renew into the second year of the programme ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for the Yenching Scholarship?** Applicants worldwide with, or on track to complete, a bachelor's degree in any field. **Is it fully funded?** Yes — tuition, accommodation, travel stipend, living stipend, and medical insurance are all covered. **How international is the cohort?** International students make up roughly 75-80% of each class. **Is there an age limit?** No strict cutoff, though the average admitted age is around 23. **Can Mainland Chinese students apply?** They follow a separate, undergraduate-only PKU pathway rather than this specific route. **How long does the programme last?** Two years, with the second year renewable based on good academic standing. ## Apply Now Apply online through the official Yenching Academy admissions page: https://yenchingacademy.pku.edu.cn/ADMISSIONS.htm
