Yenching Scholarship (Yenching Academy of Peking University)

Introduction

The Yenching Academy of Peking University offers a fully funded two-year interdisciplinary Master's in China Studies, drawing a genuinely international cohort, international students make up roughly 75-80% of each class, making it one of the most globally diverse graduate programmes based in China.

About the Opportunity

Scholars pursue an interdisciplinary Master's in China Studies, with the first year guaranteed and the second year renewable based on good academic standing, optionally connecting with faculty for supervision interest before formally applying.

Benefits

Scholars receive full tuition, on-campus accommodation, a round-trip travel stipend between their home city and Beijing, a monthly living stipend, and medical insurance.

Who Can Apply?

Applicants worldwide holding, or on track to complete by 31 August, a bachelor's degree in any field, with strong academic performance, leadership or community engagement, and English proficiency can apply; Mainland Chinese applicants follow a separate PKU undergraduate-only pathway.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The programme spans China Studies, social sciences, humanities, and economics within its interdisciplinary structure.

Required Documents

Applicants need an online application, transcripts, a degree certificate or proof of enrollment, a personal statement, recommendation letters, and English proficiency test scores sent directly by the testing body.

Application Process

Applicants apply online via the Yenching Academy application portal in English by the deadline, optionally contacting faculty about supervision interest in advance.

Deadline

Please refer to the official website for the current cycle's specific deadline, typically around 1 December.

Why You Should Apply

For graduates worldwide seeking a genuinely international, fully funded China Studies experience, Yenching Academy offers comprehensive funding and a cohort majority-composed of international peers, rare among China-based graduate programmes.

For full details, visit the official Yenching Academy admissions page: https://yenchingacademy.pku.edu.cn/ADMISSIONS.htm
