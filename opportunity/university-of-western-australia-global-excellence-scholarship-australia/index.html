University of Western Australia Global Excellence Scholarship

Automatic tuition-fee discount for high-achieving international students (ATAR 85+ or WAM 65+) admitted to an eligible UWA undergraduate or postgraduate coursework degree. No separate scholarship application — eligibility is assessed automatically upon course admission. Deadline is tied to your course application/intake date (e.g. ~30 Jul 2026 for Semester 2 2026 entry) rather than a fixed scholarship deadline.
