University of Western Australia Global Excellence Scholarship

## Introduction

The University of Western Australia Global Excellence Scholarship offers international undergraduate and postgraduate students partial to full tuition fee reductions to study at UWA, one of Australia's Group of Eight research-intensive universities based in Perth. Unlike scholarships that cover living costs and stipends, this award is specifically a tuition fee reduction, applied across the duration of your degree, making it most relevant to students weighing the overall cost of international study in Australia.

## Opportunity Overview

- **Organization:** University of Western Australia - **Country of Study:** Australia (Perth) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Partially Funded - **Degree Level:** Bachelor's or Master's - **Duration:** Length of the chosen degree programme - **Official Website:** uwa.edu.au

## Benefits

- A partial to full tuition fee reduction, depending on the specific award tier you are granted - The reduction applies for the full duration of your degree, not just the first year

## Eligibility Criteria

- Be an international undergraduate or postgraduate student - Demonstrate high academic achievement - Be admitted, or in the process of applying, to an eligible programme at UWA

## Required Documents

- Academic transcripts - Proof of your UWA admission offer - English proficiency evidence - Passport copy

## Application Process

**Step 1.** Apply for admission to your chosen undergraduate or postgraduate programme at UWA.

**Step 2.** Depending on your specific intake, you may be automatically considered for the Global Excellence Scholarship, or you may need to submit a separate scholarship application — check the current intake's specific process.

**Step 3.** Submit your transcripts, admission offer, English proficiency evidence, and passport copy as required.

**Step 4.** UWA reviews your academic record against the scholarship's excellence criteria.

**Step 5.** Successful applicants receive confirmation of their specific tuition fee reduction tier.

## Important Dates

Please refer to the official website for the current intake's specific dates, since automatic consideration versus separate application can vary by intake period.

## Selection Criteria

Selection is based primarily on high academic achievement demonstrated through your prior transcripts, assessed against UWA's specific excellence thresholds for the Global Excellence Scholarship.

## Tips for Applicants

- Check whether your specific intake requires a separate scholarship application or automatic consideration, since this varies - Make sure your transcripts clearly reflect strong, consistent academic performance - Confirm your English proficiency evidence meets UWA's specific requirements before applying - Apply for admission as early as possible, since scholarship consideration is often linked to your admission timeline - Understand that this is a tuition reduction, not a living stipend, so budget accordingly for other costs

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Who can apply for the UWA Global Excellence Scholarship?** International undergraduate or postgraduate students with high academic achievement.

**Is it fully funded?** No, it is partially funded — a tuition fee reduction ranging from partial to full, depending on the tier.

**Do I need a separate application?** This depends on your specific intake; some are automatic, others require a separate scholarship application.

**What degree levels are covered?** Both bachelor's and master's level programmes.

**Does it cover living costs?** No, it specifically reduces tuition fees, not living expenses.

**How is the tier determined?** Based on your academic achievement relative to UWA's excellence criteria for the scholarship.

## Apply Now

Check your specific intake's process on the official UWA Global Excellence Scholarship page: https://www.uwa.edu.au/study/global-excellence-scholarship
