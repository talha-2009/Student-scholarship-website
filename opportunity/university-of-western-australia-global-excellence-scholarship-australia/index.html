University of Western Australia Global Excellence Scholarship

Introduction

The University of Western Australia Global Excellence Scholarship offers international undergraduate and postgraduate students partial to full tuition fee reductions to study at UWA, one of Australia's Group of Eight research-intensive universities based in Perth. Unlike scholarships that cover living costs and stipends, this award is specifically a tuition fee reduction, applied across the duration of a student's degree.

About the Opportunity

Students admitted to UWA are considered for this scholarship depending on the specific intake, with some students automatically assessed as part of their admission application and others needing a separate scholarship application, so checking the current intake's specific process matters before assuming automatic consideration.

Benefits

Recipients receive a partial to full tuition fee reduction depending on the specific award tier, applied for the duration of their degree.

Who Can Apply?

International undergraduate or postgraduate students of high academic achievement admitted to an eligible programme at UWA can apply.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The scholarship covers all fields available at UWA, since eligibility is based on academic achievement rather than a specific discipline.

Required Documents

Applicants need academic transcripts, proof of their admission offer, English proficiency evidence, and a passport copy.

Application Process

Applicants apply for admission to UWA; depending on the specific intake, eligible students are either automatically considered for the Global Excellence Scholarship or need to submit a separate scholarship application.

Deadline

Please refer to the official website for the current intake's specific deadline, since timing depends on the admission cycle.

Why You Should Apply

For high-achieving international students considering UWA, this scholarship offers meaningful tuition relief, though confirming whether automatic or separate application applies to your specific intake is an important early step.

For full details, visit the official UWA Global Excellence Scholarship page: https://www.uwa.edu.au/study/global-excellence-scholarship
