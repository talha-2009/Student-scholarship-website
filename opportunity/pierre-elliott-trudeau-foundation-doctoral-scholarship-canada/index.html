Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Doctoral Scholarship

## Introduction The Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation Doctoral Scholarship is one of Canada's most prestigious PhD awards for the humanities and social sciences, named after the former Canadian prime minister and built around a strong emphasis on public engagement rather than academic research alone. It is open to doctoral students at Canadian or foreign universities, and its selection criteria specifically look for candidates who intend to connect their scholarly work to broader public and civic life, not simply pursue an academic career in isolation. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation - **Country of Study:** Canada (or a foreign university, depending on enrollment) - **Opportunity Type:** Scholarship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** PhD - **Duration:** Up to three years - **Official Website:** trudeaufoundation.ca ## Benefits - Up to CAD 60,000 per year for up to three years - Access to the Foundation's mentorship network - Participation in Foundation events connecting scholars with public and civic leaders ## Eligibility Criteria - Be a doctoral student in the humanities or social sciences - Be enrolled at a Canadian or foreign university - Demonstrate leadership and a genuine commitment to public engagement, not solely academic achievement ## Required Documents - Application form - A research proposal - CV - Academic transcripts - Recommendation letters ## Application Process **Step 1.** Confirm you are enrolled in, or admitted to, a PhD programme in the humanities or social sciences at an eligible university. **Step 2.** Prepare your research proposal, CV, and transcripts according to the Foundation's application requirements. **Step 3.** Secure your recommendation letters, ideally from academics who can speak to both your research potential and your public engagement. **Step 4.** Submit your complete application through the Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation's online scholarship portal by the published deadline. **Step 5.** Shortlisted candidates may be invited for further review or interview, depending on the current cycle's process. **Step 6.** Successful scholars receive funding confirmation and are welcomed into the Foundation's mentorship network. ## Important Dates - **Application deadline:** See the deadline listed for this opportunity (typically mid-November) - **Results and funding start:** Following the review process, generally aligned with the following academic year Always confirm the exact current-cycle deadline on the official Foundation website. ## Selection Criteria The Foundation looks for strong doctoral research potential in the humanities or social sciences, combined with clear evidence of leadership and a genuine intention to engage with public issues beyond academia — this dual focus distinguishes it from purely academic doctoral funding. ## Tips for Applicants - Use your research proposal and supporting materials to show a clear link between your academic work and broader public relevance, since this is central to the Foundation's mission - Choose recommenders who can speak to your leadership and civic engagement, not only your academic ability - Apply whether you're studying in Canada or abroad, since both are eligible - Be specific about how you intend to use the mentorship network and Foundation events, not just the funding itself - Submit well before the deadline, since the Foundation reviews applications thoroughly across multiple criteria ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for the Trudeau Foundation Doctoral Scholarship?** PhD students in the humanities or social sciences at Canadian or foreign universities. **Is it fully funded?** It provides up to CAD 60,000 per year for up to three years. **Do I need to study in Canada?** No, you can be enrolled at a foreign university and still be eligible. **What makes this scholarship distinctive?** Its strong emphasis on public engagement and leadership alongside academic research. **How long does the funding last?** Up to three years. **Is there a mentorship component?** Yes, scholars gain access to the Foundation's mentorship network and events. ## Apply Now Submit your application through the official Pierre Elliott Trudeau Foundation scholarship portal: https://www.trudeaufoundation.ca/scholarship/
