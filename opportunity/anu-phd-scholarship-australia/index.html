ANU PhD Scholarship

The Australian National University (ANU) offers this stipend scholarship to Higher Degree by Research candidates undertaking a PhD or Professional Doctorate by Research across any discipline. Open to both domestic and international applicants, the award provides a tax-free stipend of AUD 39,069 per annum for 3.5 years, paid fortnightly. Recipients also receive travel and removal allowances for relocating to Canberra, a thesis allowance, a dependent child allowance for international students, and paid medical and parental leave. No separate scholarship application is required: all eligible candidates who apply for admission to an ANU PhD or Professional Doctorate by Research program are automatically considered. Applicants need a Bachelor's degree with first-class honours (or upper second-class honours in some Colleges) or a Master's degree with a research component from a recognised university. International applicants must apply for admission by the Round 1 international deadline of 31 August (for commencement the following January-March); a smaller Round 2 closes 15 April for later-year starts. Because award is tied to admission timing, prospective scholars are strongly encouraged to contact a prospective ANU supervisor or the relevant Graduate Studies Convener well before submitting, since a well-matched supervisor and refined research proposal both strengthen the ranking outcome. This scholarship is one of the most accessible pathways into Australia's research sector for talented graduates worldwide, with extension options available for Indigenous candidates or those with significant industry engagement.
