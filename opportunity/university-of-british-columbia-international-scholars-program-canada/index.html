University of British Columbia International Scholars Program

A need-and-merit-based scholarship program for international undergraduate students entering UBC directly from secondary school (or transferring after first year). Offered through four award tracks (Karen McKellin International Leader of Tomorrow, Donald A. Wehrung International Student Award, International Impact Award, and Vantage Excellence), it covers tuition, fees, and living costs based on demonstrated financial need, and is renewable for up to three additional years. Applicants must demonstrate superior academic achievement, require meaningful financial support to attend UBC, and be nominated by their school or a non-profit organization. Applications for the 2027 intake will open in September 2026, with the nomination/application deadline typically falling in mid-November (historically November 15) and the UBC admissions deadline shortly after (historically December 1) — exact 2027 dates not yet confirmed by UBC.
