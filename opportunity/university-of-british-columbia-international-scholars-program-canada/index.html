University of British Columbia International Scholars Program

Introduction

The University of British Columbia International Scholars Program recognises outstanding international students entering UBC undergraduate programmes with renewable tuition awards that can significantly reduce the cost of studying at one of Canada's top research universities. Most awards within the programme are considered automatically based on your admission application, though a small number of specific awards, such as the International Major Entrance Scholarship, require nomination or an additional application step.

About the Opportunity

Students admitted to UBC undergraduate programmes are automatically considered for most International Scholars Program awards, with the university evaluating academic excellence and personal achievement as part of the standard admission review rather than a separate scholarship process. A small number of specific named awards operate outside this automatic system and require additional nomination, so students should check whether their profile matches any of these more targeted opportunities.

Benefits

Recipients receive renewable tuition awards ranging from partial funding to full tuition coverage for up to four years, depending on the specific award tier assigned.

Who Can Apply?

Outstanding international students admitted to UBC undergraduate programmes are automatically considered, selected based on academic excellence and personal achievement; some awards require separate nomination or application.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The programme covers all undergraduate fields available at UBC, since eligibility is based on overall academic and personal merit rather than a specific discipline.

Required Documents

Applicants need their UBC admission application, academic transcripts, a personal profile or essay, and evidence of English proficiency.

Application Process

Applicants apply for undergraduate admission to UBC; eligible applicants are automatically considered for most International Scholars Program awards based on that same application, with no separate scholarship form typically required.

Deadline

Please refer to the official website for the current admission cycle's specific deadline, since scholarship consideration is tied directly to UBC's undergraduate admission timeline.

Why You Should Apply

For outstanding international students targeting UBC, the largely automatic nature of this scholarship consideration means a strong admission application alone can unlock meaningful tuition support, without the added burden of a separate scholarship application process.

For full details, visit the official UBC International Scholars Program page: https://you.ubc.ca/financial-planning/scholarships-awards-international-students/international-scholars/
