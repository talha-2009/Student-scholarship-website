Stanford Data Science Summer Institute

## Introduction

The Stanford Data Science Summer Institute (SDSSI) is a funded summer research programme introducing undergraduates — with explicit priority for students from groups underrepresented in data science and computing — to real data science research alongside Stanford faculty. Unlike a paid corporate internship, this is a mentored academic research experience, aimed at broadening participation in a field where certain groups remain significantly underrepresented.

## Opportunity Overview

- **Organization:** Stanford University - **Country of Study:** United States (Stanford campus) - **Opportunity Type:** Summer School - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** Undergraduate - **Duration:** A structured summer research period - **Official Website:** sdssi.stanford.edu

## Benefits

- Full funding for housing - Full funding for travel - A stipend - A mentored research experience with Stanford faculty

## Eligibility Criteria

- Be an undergraduate student - Priority is given to students from groups underrepresented in data science and computing

## Required Documents

- Transcript - Resume - A statement of interest - A recommendation letter

## Application Process

**Step 1.** Apply online through the SDSSI application portal.

**Step 2.** Submit your transcript, resume, and statement of interest.

**Step 3.** Arrange a recommendation letter to support your application.

**Step 4.** SDSSI reviews applications with explicit attention to broadening participation in data science.

**Step 5.** Successful applicants are matched with a Stanford faculty mentor and begin the funded summer research programme.

## Important Dates

Please refer to the official website for the current year's specific application deadline, since SDSSI runs on an annual cycle.

## Selection Criteria

SDSSI evaluates candidates on genuine interest and potential in data science, academic background, and fit with the programme's explicit mission of increasing participation from underrepresented groups in data science and computing.

## Tips for Applicants

- Use your statement of interest to explain specifically why data science and why now, connecting it to your academic background and goals - If you belong to a group underrepresented in data science or computing, note this explicitly, since it is a stated priority for the programme - Choose a recommender who can speak to your academic potential and readiness for research, even if you haven't yet taken advanced data science coursework - Apply as early as the current cycle allows, since strong summer research programmes can be competitive - Research Stanford faculty's current data science work in advance to strengthen your statement of interest

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Who can apply for SDSSI?** Undergraduate students, with priority given to those from groups underrepresented in data science and computing.

**Is it fully funded?** Yes — housing, travel, and a stipend are all covered.

**Do I need prior data science experience?** Not necessarily; the programme is designed to introduce participants to data science research.

**Where is it held?** On the Stanford University campus.

**What will I do during the programme?** Conduct mentored data science research alongside Stanford faculty.

**How is priority for underrepresented groups applied?** It is an explicit factor in selection, though specific criteria are detailed on the official application page.

## Apply Now

Apply through the official Stanford Data Science Summer Institute portal: https://sdssi.stanford.edu/
