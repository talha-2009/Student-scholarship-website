Stanford Data Science Summer Institute

Introduction

Stanford's Data Science Summer Institute addresses a well-documented gap in the tech pipeline: data science and computing remain fields where certain groups are significantly underrepresented, and SDSSI exists specifically to give undergraduates from those groups genuine, funded research exposure to the field before graduate school or industry hiring decisions are made.

About the Opportunity

Participants spend the summer conducting mentored data science research directly with Stanford faculty, working on real research questions rather than a simplified training curriculum, giving students authentic exposure to what graduate-level or industry data science research actually involves. The explicit priority given to underrepresented students shapes both recruitment and selection, distinguishing SDSSI from more generally competitive summer research programmes.

Benefits

Participants receive full funding for housing, travel, and a stipend, along with a mentored research experience directly with Stanford faculty throughout the summer.

Who Can Apply?

Undergraduate students can apply, with explicit priority given to those from groups underrepresented in data science and computing.

Fields of Study or Available Areas

The programme centers specifically on data science, spanning statistical methods, machine learning, and applied computational research depending on the specific faculty mentor and project.

Required Documents

Applicants need a transcript, a resume, a statement of interest, and a recommendation letter.

Application Process

Applicants apply online through the SDSSI application portal with transcripts and a statement of interest explaining their background and motivation for pursuing data science research.

Deadline

Please refer to the official website for the current cycle's specific deadline, since SDSSI runs on an annual cycle with dates that can shift year to year.

Why You Should Apply

For undergraduates from underrepresented backgrounds genuinely interested in data science, SDSSI offers a rare, funded opportunity to work directly with Stanford faculty on real research, a credential and experience that can meaningfully shape future graduate school or career decisions in the field.

For full application details, visit the official SDSSI website: https://sdssi.stanford.edu/
