BMW Group Change Maker Fellowship

A fully funded fellowship co-created by BMW Group and ESMT Berlin for purpose-driven future leaders, covering full tuition (and in some cases a living stipend) for an ESMT graduate management programme. Currently in its fifth year, the fellowship is available across three ESMT programmes: the Full-time MBA (January 2027 intake), the MSc in Innovation and Entrepreneurship (September 2026 intake), and the Global Online MBA (October 2026 intake). Open to applicants of any nationality who have secured (or are applying for) admission to an eligible ESMT programme; selection focuses on leadership potential, social impact orientation, and alignment with BMW Group's purpose and values. Deadlines vary by programme and intake — visit the official fellowship page for current-cycle deadlines for each programme.
