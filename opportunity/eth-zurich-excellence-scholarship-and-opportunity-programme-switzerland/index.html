ETH Zurich Excellence Scholarship and Opportunity Programme

A merit-based, fully funded scholarship for exceptional students admitted to a Master's programme at ETH Zurich, one of the world's top universities for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Covers full tuition fees, a monthly living stipend of CHF 1,150, and access to a personal mentoring programme for the duration of the Master's degree (typically 1.5–2 years). Open to international and domestic students who have been admitted (or are applying for admission) to any ETH Zurich Master's programme; candidates must be nominated by their department or apply directly through the scholarship portal. Applications for the 2027 intake open November 1, 2026 and close November 30, 2026 — applicants must simultaneously apply for admission to their chosen Master's programme.
