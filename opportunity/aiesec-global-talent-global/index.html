AIESEC Global Talent

## Introduction AIESEC has spent decades building a global network of student and youth organizations, and Global Talent is the platform through which that network connects young people with paid international internships posted directly by corporate partners around the world. For master's students who need to remain enrolled during an internship, or recent bachelor's graduates within two months of finishing their degree, Global Talent offers a structured, supported way into international work experience that goes beyond simply applying cold to companies abroad. ## About the Opportunity Rather than running a single program with one set of dates, Global Talent functions as a live marketplace of internship listings posted by AIESEC's corporate partners across business, technology, marketing, engineering, and finance. Each listing comes from a specific employer with its own role, location, and duration, meaning the actual day-to-day experience varies considerably depending on which opportunity a candidate applies to and is selected for. What AIESEC adds on top of the listings themselves is structured support: cultural preparation before departure, help navigating visa and travel logistics, and mandatory insurance coverage arranged on the intern's behalf. ## Benefits Global Talent internships are paid, with compensation set by the specific host employer and role, and can run anywhere from three to eighteen months depending on the listing. Interns receive cultural preparation designed to help them adjust to working in an unfamiliar country, along with visa and travel support that reduces much of the administrative burden interns would otherwise handle alone. AIESEC also arranges mandatory life and health insurance coverage for every intern, a safeguard that is not always guaranteed in independently-arranged international internships. ## Who Can Apply? Two groups are eligible: master's students who will remain enrolled in their program throughout the internship, and bachelor's graduates within two months of their graduation date. Beyond that enrollment or graduation timing, eligibility depends heavily on the specific listing a candidate applies to, since individual corporate partners set their own additional requirements around field of study, language skills, or prior experience. ## Fields of Study or Available Areas Listings span business, technology, marketing, engineering, and finance, reflecting the breadth of AIESEC's corporate partner network. Because new listings appear continuously, the exact mix of available fields shifts over time, so candidates are best served by checking the platform directly for opportunities matching their background. ## Required Documents Applicants need a completed AIESEC platform profile, a CV, and proof of their current enrollment or recent graduation. A valid passport is also required to support visa arrangements once a candidate is matched with a specific internship. ## Application Process Candidates start by creating a profile on the AIESEC platform, then search listed opportunities by field and location to find roles matching their interests and eligibility. After applying to specific listings, candidates go through that particular partner organization's own selection process, which can include interviews or additional assessments depending on the employer. Once selected, AIESEC coordinates cultural preparation, visa support, and insurance arrangements ahead of the intern's departure. ## Deadline Because Global Talent listings are posted on a rolling basis by many different corporate partners rather than following a single annual cycle, there is no fixed application deadline. Candidates should check the platform regularly, since strong listings in competitive fields or locations can close once enough applications come in. ## Why You Should Apply For students and recent graduates who want genuine international work experience but lack an existing network of contacts abroad, Global Talent lowers the practical barriers that usually make international internships hard to arrange independently: visa logistics, cultural preparation, and insurance are all handled through AIESEC rather than left entirely to the individual. Among paid internship and global opportunity programs aimed at students, Global Talent stands out for the sheer scale and international reach of its partner network, making it worth checking regularly even for candidates without a specific country or company already in mind. To browse current listings and start an application, visit the official AIESEC Global Talent platform: https://aiesec.org/global-talent
