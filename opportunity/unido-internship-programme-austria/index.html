UNIDO Internship Programme

Internships with the UN Industrial Development Organization, primarily at its Vienna headquarters, typically lasting 3-6 months. Open to students enrolled in or who have recently completed a relevant graduate degree. Applications accepted on a rolling basis via the UNIDO careers portal.
