Chevening Scholarship

[2026/27 cycle CLOSED — deadline was 7 Oct 2025] UK government scholarship for future leaders, funding a one-year Master's at any UK university. Covers tuition, monthly stipend, and travel. Requires 2+ years (2,800 hours) of work experience. The 2027/28 cycle is expected to open around August 2026 with a deadline in early October 2026, but the official date has not yet been confirmed — check chevening.org directly.
