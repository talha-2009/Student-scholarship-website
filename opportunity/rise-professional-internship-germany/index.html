RISE Professional Internship

## Introduction RISE Professional is DAAD's advanced counterpart to RISE Germany, aimed at graduate students and PhD candidates rather than undergraduates, and focused specifically on placements at German industrial companies and research institutes with a clear industry orientation. It suits students who want direct exposure to how German industry applies research in practice, complete with support for the practical logistics — including a work-permit waiver arranged by DAAD — that graduate-level international interns often face. ## Opportunity Overview - **Organization:** DAAD (German Academic Exchange Service) - **Country of Study:** Germany - **Opportunity Type:** Internship - **Funding Type:** Fully Funded - **Degree Level:** Master's or PhD - **Duration:** Varies by project, must begin on or after May 15 - **Official Website:** daad.de/rise ## Benefits - A monthly stipend to cover living costs - Health, accident, and liability insurance - A work-permit waiver arranged directly by DAAD, simplifying the visa/work-authorization process - Partial reimbursement of German language course fees ## Eligibility Criteria - Be a graduate student or PhD candidate from the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, or Ireland - Be seeking a research internship at a German industrial company or research institute with an industry focus - Be available for an internship beginning on or after May 15 of the relevant year ## Required Documents - Online application form - Official academic transcript - Certificate of enrollment - A motivation letter tailored to each specific project - A reference letter with official letterhead and signature ## Application Process **Step 1.** Browse the RISE Professional internship database for listed positions at German industrial companies and research institutes. **Step 2.** Write a tailored motivation letter for each specific project you apply to. **Step 3.** Submit your transcript, certificate of enrollment, and signed reference letter alongside your application. **Step 4.** If a host company or institute selects you, you will receive a formal offer specifying start date and stipend. **Step 5.** Confirm your internship start date falls on or after May 15, as required by the programme. ## Important Dates - **Application deadline:** See the deadline listed for this opportunity (typically 30 November) - **Host selection:** Communicated by individual host companies/institutes over the following months - **Internship period:** Must start on or after May 15 of the relevant year ## Selection Criteria Individual host companies and research institutes select their own interns based on how well your graduate-level academic background and stated motivation match their specific industrial research project. ## Tips for Applicants - Apply to multiple listed projects, since selection happens at the individual host level, not centrally - Tailor each motivation letter specifically to the host company's industrial focus, showing genuine interest in their applied research area - Confirm you can start on or after May 15, since this is a fixed programme requirement - Highlight any prior industry-relevant research or coursework clearly in your application - Use the work-permit waiver arranged by DAAD to simplify your own visa planning, rather than pursuing a separate work permit independently ## Frequently Asked Questions **Who can apply for RISE Professional?** Graduate students and PhD candidates from the US, Canada, UK, or Ireland. **Is RISE Professional fully funded?** Yes — it includes a monthly stipend, insurance, and a DAAD-arranged work-permit waiver. **Where are placements based?** At German industrial companies and research institutes with an industry focus. **When can the internship start?** Only on or after May 15 of the relevant year. **Do I need to speak German?** Not necessarily, though partial reimbursement is available for German language courses. **Can undergraduate students apply?** No, RISE Professional is specifically for graduate students and PhD candidates. ## Apply Now Browse current listed positions and apply through the official RISE Professional database: https://www.daad.de/rise/en/rise-professional/find-an-internship/
