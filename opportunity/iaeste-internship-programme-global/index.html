IAESTE Internship Programme

IAESTE (International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience) places STEM students in paid, supervised technical internships abroad through a global network of national committees in over 80 countries. Internships typically run 6 to 12 weeks (some up to 12 months) and include a living-cost-covering stipend, with placements in engineering, natural sciences, computer science, architecture, and agriculture. Applicants must be enrolled in a relevant Bachelor's or Master's program at a university with an active IAESTE national committee; applications and placement cycles run on a rolling basis throughout the year via each country's national committee, with most exchanges placed for the following summer.
