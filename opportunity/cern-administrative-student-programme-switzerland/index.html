CERN Administrative Student Programme

A 2–12 month internship at CERN in Geneva for undergraduate students studying administration, HR, finance, law, communications, logistics, or related fields. Students gain hands-on experience supporting real operations at one of the world's leading scientific research centres, receiving a monthly net allowance of CHF 3,486 plus a travel allowance and comprehensive health insurance. Applicants must be nationals of a CERN Member or Associate Member State, have completed at least 18 months (3 semesters) of undergraduate study, and remain enrolled as full-time students during the internship; STEM/engineering students should apply for the Technical Student Programme instead. The 2026-3 batch closed June 30, 2026; check careers.cern/programmes/administrative-studentship/ for the next opening date and submit your application before the next published deadline.
