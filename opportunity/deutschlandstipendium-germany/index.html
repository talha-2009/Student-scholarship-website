Scholarship • Germany

Deutschlandstipendium

National (not university-specific) merit scholarship providing EUR 300/month to high-achieving students at participating German universities, funded jointly by private sponsors and the German government. DEADLINE VARIES BY UNIVERSITY — most 2026/27 application windows fall between March and August 2026 (e.g. TUM: 18-31 May and 13-26 Jul 2026; many universities' 2026/27 windows have already closed as of this review). Next national cycle (2027/28) typically opens around March 2027 — check your target university's page directly.
