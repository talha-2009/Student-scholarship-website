Asian Institute of Technology Scholarship

Scholarships offered by the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT) in Bangkok for international students pursuing Master's or doctoral programmes in engineering, environment, and management. Awards range from partial tuition waivers to full tuition coverage depending on the scholarship category (AIT Fellowship covers full tuition; other awards cover portions), but do not include living costs — recipients are responsible for accommodation and daily expenses. Open to international students from any country applying to an AIT degree programme; applicants are automatically considered for available scholarships upon submitting a complete admissions application. AIT admits students twice yearly (January and August intakes); scholarship application deadlines align with admissions deadlines, typically around June 10 for the August intake and December 20 for the January intake — confirm exact dates on the official AIT scholarships page.
