About OpportunityNest

OpportunityNest helps students find real scholarships, internships, and fellowships across multiple countries worldwide. The goal is simple: make it easier to discover useful opportunities without jumping between dozens of websites.

Scholarship and career information is often scattered, duplicated, outdated, or difficult to compare. OpportunityNest centralizes verified opportunity listings so students can spend more time preparing strong applications and less time searching.

Listings are reviewed and updated regularly, but OpportunityNest is independent and is not affiliated with any government, university, scholarship provider, or employer. Applicants should always confirm details on the official program website before applying.
