About OpportunityNest

OpportunityNest helps students find real scholarships, internships, and fellowships across multiple countries worldwide. The goal is simple: make it easier to discover useful opportunities without jumping between dozens of websites.

Scholarship and career information is often scattered, duplicated, outdated, or difficult to compare. OpportunityNest centralizes verified opportunity listings so students can spend more time preparing strong applications and less time searching.

Listings are reviewed and updated regularly, but OpportunityNest is independent and is not affiliated with any government, university, scholarship provider, or employer. Applicants should always confirm details on the official program website before applying.

Our Editorial Process

Every listing on OpportunityNest goes through a structured review process before publication:

  1. Source verification — We only list opportunities from recognized universities, governments, international organizations, and established foundations. Each listing links directly to the official program page.
  2. Deadline monitoring — Deadlines are checked against official sources. Expired listings are automatically removed from search results.
  3. Eligibility cross-checking — Funding details, eligibility criteria, and application requirements are compared with the official announcement before publication.
  4. Regular updates — Listings are reviewed periodically. If a program changes its deadline, funding, or eligibility, we update the listing or flag it for review.
  5. Error correction — If you spot an inaccuracy, use our Contact page to report it. We review and correct verified errors within 48 hours.

Who Runs OpportunityNest

OpportunityNest is an independent project built by students and early-career professionals who understand how frustrating it is to find reliable scholarship and internship information. We are not a government agency, university, or scholarship provider. We do not process applications, collect fees, or make funding decisions.

Our role is strictly informational: we aggregate publicly available opportunity data into one searchable platform so students can discover programs faster and apply with confidence.

What We Cover

  • Scholarships — Fully funded and partial scholarships for undergraduate, master's, and PhD students
  • Internships — Paid and unpaid placements at international organizations, NGOs, and corporations
  • Fellowships — Research, professional development, and leadership programmes
  • Competitions — Global contests with prizes, funding, and mentorship opportunities
  • Youth Programs — Exchange programmes, summits, and capacity-building initiatives

Opportunities span countries including Australia, Austria, Canada, Germany, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, the United States, and more.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not process applications. Every listing links to the official programme website where you apply directly.
  • We do not collect fees. OpportunityNest is free for all users. We never charge for access to listings or application guidance.
  • We do not make funding decisions. We are not a scholarship provider, university, or government agency.
  • We do not guarantee accuracy. While we review every listing, deadlines and eligibility can change. Always verify on the official website.
  • We do not sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Important Disclaimer

While we strive for accuracy, OpportunityNest does not guarantee that every detail on a listing is current. Deadlines, eligibility criteria, and funding amounts can change without notice. Always verify information on the official program website before submitting an application. See our full disclaimer for details.
