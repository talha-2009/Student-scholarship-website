Partially Funded Scholarships

Partially Funded Scholarships on OpportunityNest bring together programs where funding is a primary filter. This collection currently contains 7 matching opportunities. Each listing states the funding level, eligible countries, field, deadline, and the official source. This page is assembled for students combining merit awards with savings, sponsorship, assistantships, or other financial support. Applicants can compare funding arrangements side by side and decide which programs align with their financial needs.

7 matching listings.

What to check before applying

Applicants should verify that the funding covers realistic costs for their situation. Important points include reduced tuition, fee waivers, one-time grants, living allowances, or targeted support for specific study expenses. Also review academic merit, course admission, financial circumstances where relevant, nationality, and award-specific conditions to confirm fit before investing time in the application. Check whether the funding is renewable, covers dependents, or requires a separate work or teaching obligation.

A practical workflow

calculate the remaining funding gap, confirm whether awards can be combined, prepare evidence, and submit through the provider. Build a schedule that accounts for document preparation, reference requests, language tests, and any nomination procedures. Keep the provider's official page as your primary reference throughout the process. Create a checklist of required documents and verify each deadline in the provider's time zone.

Improving your results

compare net cost after the award, check renewal conditions, and secure a realistic plan for expenses the scholarship excludes. Compare each listing against your actual funding needs and eligibility. Programs that fit well are usually more competitive than those submitted broadly. Focus your preparation time on the programs that offer the best match for your profile and goals.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How many partially funded scholarships are currently listed?

OpportunityNest currently shows 7 matching listings on this page. This number changes when verified opportunities are added or removed from the database.

How do I know which partially funded scholarships to prioritize?

Compare listings by deadline proximity, funding level, and how well they match your academic background or career goals. Starting with programs whose deadlines are furthest out gives you more preparation time.

Does partially funded scholarships cover all expenses?

Read the funding details on each listing. Some awards cover tuition alone, while others include living stipends, travel, and health insurance.

Can I apply for multiple partially funded scholarships at the same time?

Yes, as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each program. Be aware that some providers may restrict concurrent awards.
