Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

What Information Is Collected

At this time, OpportunityNest only collects information you choose to submit through the contact form, such as your name, email address, and message.

How Information Is Used

Contact form information is used only to read and respond to your message. OpportunityNest does not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

OpportunityNest uses Supabase to store and display opportunity listings, and Vercel to host the website. The contact form uses Formspree to deliver messages. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies

OpportunityNest does not use tracking cookies at this time.

Privacy Questions

For privacy questions, please use the Contact page. See also our Terms & Conditions.
