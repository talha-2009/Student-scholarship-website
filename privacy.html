Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

What Information Is Collected

At this time, OpportunityNest only collects information you choose to submit through the contact form, such as your name, email address, and message.

How Information Is Used

Contact form information is used only to read and respond to your message. OpportunityNest does not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

OpportunityNest uses Supabase to store and display opportunity listings, and Vercel to host the website. The contact form uses Formspree to deliver messages. Google Analytics helps us understand site usage, and Google AdSense provides and measures advertising. These services may process device, usage, and contact-form data according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies

OpportunityNest and its third-party providers may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, advertising, fraud prevention, and essential site operation. Google may use cookies to serve, personalize, and measure ads where permitted by applicable law and the consent choices available to you. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

Privacy Questions

For privacy questions, please use the Contact page. See also our Terms & Conditions.
