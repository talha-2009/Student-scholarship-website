Frequently Asked Questions

What is OpportunityNest?

OpportunityNest lists real scholarships, internships, and fellowships across multiple countries worldwide.

Is OpportunityNest affiliated with any government or university?

No. OpportunityNest is independent. Applicants apply directly through each program's official website.

Is it free to use OpportunityNest?

Yes. Browsing and searching OpportunityNest is completely free.

How often is the scholarship data updated?

Listings are reviewed periodically. Users should confirm deadlines on the official link before applying.

How do I apply for a scholarship I find here?

Each listing has a "View & Apply" link to the official page. OpportunityNest does not process applications.

Why don't I see scholarships for my country?

The site features opportunities from multiple countries. New countries are added as listings become available.

I found an error in a listing - what do I do?

Please report it through the Contact page.

Do you guarantee I will get the scholarship if I apply?

No. OpportunityNest only provides information. Decisions are made by the scholarship provider or university.
