Frequently Asked Questions

What is OpportunityNest?

OpportunityNest is a free, independent platform that aggregates scholarships, internships, fellowships, competitions, and youth programmes from universities, governments, and international organisations worldwide. We centralise verified listings so students can discover, compare, and apply to opportunities without searching across dozens of websites.

Is OpportunityNest affiliated with any government or university?

No. OpportunityNest is completely independent. We are not affiliated with any government, university, scholarship provider, or employer. All applications are submitted directly through each programme's official website. We act only as an information aggregator.

Is it free to use OpportunityNest?

Yes. Browsing, searching, and using all features on OpportunityNest is completely free. We do not charge any fees, require registration, or ask for payment. You will never be asked to pay to view or apply for a listing through our platform.

How often is the opportunity data updated?

Listings are reviewed and updated regularly. Expired opportunities are automatically removed from search results. However, deadlines and eligibility criteria can change without notice, so we always recommend confirming details on the official programme website before applying.

How do I apply for a scholarship or internship I find here?

Each listing includes a direct link to the official programme page where you can submit your application. OpportunityNest does not process applications, collect fees, or make admission decisions. Simply click the "Apply Now" button on any listing to visit the official source.

Why don't I see opportunities for my country?

OpportunityNest currently features opportunities from countries including Australia, Austria, Canada, Germany, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, and the United States, with more being added regularly. If your country is not listed, check back soon or use the search function to find opportunities open to international applicants from your region.

I found an error in a listing — what do I do?

Please report it through our Contact page. Include the listing title, the URL, and the incorrect information. We review and correct verified errors within 48 hours.

Do you guarantee I will get the scholarship if I apply?

No. OpportunityNest only provides information about available opportunities. Acceptance decisions are made entirely by the scholarship provider, university, or programme organiser. We cannot influence or guarantee outcomes.

What types of opportunities are listed on OpportunityNest?

We list scholarships (fully funded and partial), internships (paid and unpaid), fellowships, research grants, competitions with prizes, and youth exchange programmes. Opportunities cover undergraduate, master's, PhD, and postdoctoral levels across all fields of study.

How can I improve my chances of being selected?

Start by reading the full eligibility criteria on the official programme page. Prepare your documents early — especially motivation letters and references. Submit your application at least 48 hours before the deadline to avoid technical issues. Tailor each application to the specific programme rather than sending generic materials. Our detail pages include tips on common mistakes to avoid.

Can I suggest an opportunity to be listed?

Yes. If you know of a scholarship, internship, or fellowship that should be on OpportunityNest, use our Contact page to submit the programme name, official URL, and a brief description. We review all suggestions.

Is my personal data safe?

OpportunityNest does not collect personal data beyond what you voluntarily submit through our contact form. We do not sell, share, or trade your information. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
