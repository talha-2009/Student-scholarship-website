Fully Funded Fellowships

Fully Funded Fellowships on OpportunityNest bring together programs where funding is a primary filter. This collection currently contains 19 matching opportunities. Each listing states the funding level, eligible countries, field, deadline, and the official source. This page is assembled for researchers, graduate students, and emerging leaders seeking fellowships with substantial financial support. Applicants can compare funding arrangements side by side and decide which programs align with their financial needs.

19 matching listings.

What to check before applying

Applicants should verify that the funding covers realistic costs for their situation. Important points include stipends, travel support, research time, mentoring, host-institution access, and international networks. Also review career stage, research or leadership focus, nationality rules, proposed outcomes, and recommendation requirements to confirm fit before investing time in the application. Check whether the funding is renewable, covers dependents, or requires a separate work or teaching obligation.

A practical workflow

verify the funding package, shape a focused proposal, secure referees, and submit through the official provider. Build a schedule that accounts for document preparation, reference requests, language tests, and any nomination procedures. Keep the provider's official page as your primary reference throughout the process. Create a checklist of required documents and verify each deadline in the provider's time zone.

Improving your results

read the funding breakdown carefully, show concrete impact, and leave time for referee coordination. Compare each listing against your actual funding needs and eligibility. Programs that fit well are usually more competitive than those submitted broadly. Focus your preparation time on the programs that offer the best match for your profile and goals.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How many fully funded fellowships are currently listed?

OpportunityNest currently shows 19 matching listings on this page. This number changes when verified opportunities are added or removed from the database.

How do I know which fully funded fellowships to prioritize?

Compare listings by deadline proximity, funding level, and how well they match your academic background or career goals. Starting with programs whose deadlines are furthest out gives you more preparation time.

Does fully funded fellowships cover all expenses?

Read the funding details on each listing. Some awards cover tuition alone, while others include living stipends, travel, and health insurance.

Can I apply for multiple fully funded fellowships at the same time?

Yes, as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each program. Be aware that some providers may restrict concurrent awards.
